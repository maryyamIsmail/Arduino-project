LED (Light Emitting Diode)

LEDs are like tiny light bulbs. They light up when you tell them to, adding color to your projects.

RESISTER

Resisters are like traffic controllers for electricity.They make sure the flow of electricity is just right, preventing any traffic jams.

BUTTONS

Buttons are like switches. Press them to make things happen, like turning on a light.
