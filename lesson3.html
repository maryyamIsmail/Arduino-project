Discovering Arduino's Tricks

Hey future coders, get ready to make some digital magic happen!
Arduino has cool tricks up its sleeve! Servo motors can move like robot arms, ultrasonic sensors measure distance like magic rulers, and potentiometers adjust things with a twist.
